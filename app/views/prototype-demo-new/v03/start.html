Dartford Crossing is a free flow crossing which means that you do not pay charges at a booth when crossing but can charges for crossing in various ways.

Learn more about Dartford Crossing & Dart Charge.

Use this service to:

  • pay for the Dartford Crossing charge
  • pay or challenge a penalty charge notice (PCN)
  • create and manage an account
  • check payment for crossings
