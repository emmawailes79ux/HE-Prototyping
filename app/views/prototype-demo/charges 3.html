Dartford Crossing
(Dart Charge)

Charges and fines

You must pay the charge if you use the Dartford Crossing between 6am and 10pm. Charges apply every day - including weekends and bank hoildays.

How much you pay to cross depends on the type of vehicle you're driving and if you already have an account.

Dart Charge contact centre

  • Phone: 0300 300 0120
  • From outside the UK: +44 (0) 300 300 0120
  • Textphone: 18001 0300 300 0120
  • Monday to Sunday, 8am to 10pm (excluding public holidays)
