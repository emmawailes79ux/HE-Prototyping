We have received the penalty charge notice (PCN) challenge for relating to the number plate

Late payment charges will be paused while your challenge is considered. The amount you owe will not increase during this time.

What happens next

You will receive a response by post to your challenge within 15 working days. This will tell you if your PCN challenge was successful.

You will no longer need to pay the PCN and there will be no additional charges or fines If your challenge is successful.

If your challenge is rejected, you will need to pay the PCN and any penalties and fines within a set amount of time. The details of how much you need to pay and when to pay will be sent to you by letter and email.

Support with money problems

If you are struggling to pay your PCN because of existing debt or money problems contact the Dart Charge team

The team will look at ways to help you avoid unmanageable debt.
